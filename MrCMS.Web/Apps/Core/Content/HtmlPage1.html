ConnectCare

The health Insurance plan that connects Nigerians in the diaspora with family and friends in the home land.

Introduction

Traditionally, most of us in diaspora are responsible for the multiple support needs of our loved ones back home and healthcare takes centre stage. The frequent experience of paying hospital bills without a means to ascertain the quality of health care, track or monitor expenses and get accurate recovery reports can be unnerving. The cycle is often repetitive without reliable feedback channels and often times, the outcomes can be fatal.

ConnectCare ® is a diaspora engagement initiative offering options of health insurance plans. It brings to you four(4) flexible health plans and reliable health care management solution on a trackable platform for monitoring the health status of your loved ones in Nigeria thereby relieving the burden of health care concern and frees up valuable time for you to attend to other task.

Wherever your loved ones are, ConnectCare ® is designed to deliver desired clinical outcomes by providing access to quality health care services. Finally, you have a health plan that carries you along, manages the health care of your loved ones and gets you involved.
